About Jaun Elia

𝕵aun Elia was a renowned Pakistani Urdu poet, philosopher, and scholar, celebrated for his unique style and deeply introspective poetry. Born on December 14, 1931, in Amroha, India, he moved to Pakistan after the partition in 1957. Jaun's work often delved into themes of love, existential angst, and a critique of societal norms, resonating with readers across generations. His remarkable fluency in multiple languages, including Arabic, Persian, and Sanskrit, enriched his literary craft. Woh gumaan bhi gaya jo umang baaqi hai.
