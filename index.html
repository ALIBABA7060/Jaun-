About Jaun Elia

ğ•µaun Elia was a renowned Pakistani Urdu poet, philosopher, and scholar, celebrated for his unique style and deeply introspective poetry. Born on December 14, 1931, in Amroha, India, he moved to Pakistan after the partition in 1957. Jaun's work often delved into themes of love, existential angst, and a critique of societal norms, resonating with readers across generations. His remarkable fluency in multiple languages, including Arabic, Persian, and Sanskrit, enriched his literary craft. Ek din kisi sheher ke virane mein

Ek din kisi sheher ke virane mein,
Dekha tha ik dhanak ko deewane mein,
Be-yaar-o-madadgaar khudi ka shaida,
Aks tha mera kisi aaine mein.

Mere hisaar mein ab koi

Mere hisaar mein ab koi rang baaqi hai,
Woh gumaan bhi gaya jo umang baaqi hai.
Mein talaash hoon apni talaash ka aalam,
Woh zubaan bhi gayi jo maang baaqi hai.
